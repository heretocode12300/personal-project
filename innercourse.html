About this course:

Video editing is not just about merging a few clips together, the real essence of editing lies in the creative process of planning, recording and combining. How good a video would turn out to be is not only dependent on the years of expertise that an editor may have but rather his creative thought process, the way he would imagine his video is the way the video would turn out to be. This course, while it does not teaches you imagination, would present a few ways and techniques that can make your editing process more efficient and increase the overall quality of your video.
