My Journey:

As a person who started editing out of love for montages albeit with no experience on how to edit whatsoever, like other editors my journey also begin with softwares like Wondershare Filmora and Inshot and while my editing for the first two years was quite mediocre, my shift from editing montages to editing informative videos was the transition that allowed me to explore various editing methods and ways to improve my editing in every possible aspect, including the use color correction, the use of SFX's, animation, Graphics, effects, transitions and much more.

Currently I stand at a point where I can call myself an expert editor but it doesn't mean that I am the best, the jourey for becoming a top of the line editor is tough, something that requires endless dedication and motivation, and while I was unable to put in that much which was required to be a renowned editor, it does not mean its impossible, just some efforts can make endless imaginations come true.

Purpose of this website

The reason why I created this website is to be able to show my work to others while ensuring that others are able to learn something out of it, the rationale as to why I created a course section on this website. The main intent behind all of this is to be able to give something away to the users, a skill that would help the users to experience something new.

These courses might not be of much value for someone who is already an expert or someone who has been editing for quitesome time, but for those who are just beginners or even those who are on an intermetidate level, those users might be able to find something of great insight out of these courses, techniques and strategies that may be able to help make their editing faster and much more engaging.
